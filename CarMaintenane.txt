Reg Maintenance:
-----------------  
every year:  
* hoses and fluids
* steering and suspension
* brake pad wear/disc check
* handbrake
* power steering fluid
* driveshaft
* exhaust
* pollen air filter
* aux drivebelt
* fuel filter

every 58K km starting at 68K km:  
* Check/Adjust Valve clearance
* Air filter (clean K&N)
* Trans Fluid check level

every 97K km starting at 106K km:  
* Timing Belt
* Aux drive belt

every 4 years :  
* Brake fluid
